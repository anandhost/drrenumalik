About

Dr. Renu Malik, passed MBBS, MD (Obst. & Gynecology) from Maulana Azad Medical College, Delhi. There after worked in Dept. of Gynecology in Safdarjung Hospital from 1991-93.

One of the leading Gynecologists of Delhi, practicing from last 30 years dealing with all kinds of gynaecological problems like prolapsed uterus, fibroid, menstrual abnormalities, inferitility, abortions, ectopic pregnancy etc. She has conducted more than 30,000 deliveries till now. She is famous for conducting painless and complicated deliveries. She has performed a variety of gyanecological surgeries such as hysterectomy, cancer surgery, cryosurgery, tubal surgeries, ovarian surgeries, etc.
